SpectraLogic T-Finity Import Guide

  • SpectraLogic SOP PDF for Download

1. Remove the stylus from the side of the center panel, near the touchscreen

2. On the touchscreen, using the stylus, select/tap "select here to log in"

3. To use the keyboard, select/tap the keyboard icon in the bottom right of the screen. The toggle to switch between letters and numbers/other characters is in the bottom right

4. In the following screen, enter the credentials below in the User and Password fields, respectively, then select/tap "Login

  • User - operator
  • Password - mtp0spectra

5. Once at the Home screen, in the let navigation pane, select/tap "Import/Export"

6. In the Import/Export screen, select/tap "Export/Exchange" for Populated Chambers in the Entry/Exit section

7. At this point, once a robot is available, the moves required to transport the TeraPack to the TAP (import/export chamber) will begin.

8. The TAP will open; remove the TeraPack, insert the tape(s), such the the barcode(s) will be oriented with the "D" character to your left. The TeraPack is slotted, so a tape can only be inserted in one direction.

9. Inser the TeraPack back into the TAP, such that the tape spindle is facing you, as seen below.

10. At this point select "Stop Exporting" to complete importing the tapes/TeraPack. An additional progress screen will ben shown. When import is complete, the Home screen is returned, and you can use the Diva Import site to re-import the tape(s) to DIVA.
